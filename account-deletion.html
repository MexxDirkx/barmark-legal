Account Deletion Request

BarMark — Last updated: April 2, 2026

You can delete your BarMark account and all associated data at any time using one of the options below. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted?

  • Your account credentials (email address)
  • Your display name and profile photo
  • All workout and fitness data (exercises, history, sets, reps, weights)
  • Routines, notes, and goals
  • Leaderboard entries and social data (follows, posts, comments)

How to delete your account

Option 1 — Delete directly in the App Fastest

Open BarMark → go to Settings → scroll down → tap "Delete account". Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Send an email to dirkxmexx@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request — BarMark" and include the email address linked to your account. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email once complete.

Note: Deletion is permanent. Once deleted, your account and all workout data cannot be recovered. If you simply want a break, you can just uninstall the app — your data will remain saved and available when you reinstall.
