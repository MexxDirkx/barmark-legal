BarMark is an individual developer project created by Mexx Dirkx, based in the Netherlands. You can contact us at: dirkxmexx@gmail.com

We collect only the data necessary to provide the App's functionality:

Data typeWhy we collect itWhere it is stored
Email addressAccount creation and authenticationFirebase Authentication (Google)

Profile photoPersonalisation within the AppFirebase Storage (Google)

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and display your workout history
  • To personalise your App experience (name, profile photo)

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for marketing or profiling purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase, a platform provided by Google LLC, for authentication, data storage, and crash reporting (Crashlytics). Firebase may process your data on Google's infrastructure in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where Google operates. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

5. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account exists. When you delete your account (either in-app or via our account deletion request page), all associated data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy law (including the GDPR for users in the EU/EEA), you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — update inaccurate data (directly within the App)
  • Deletion — delete your account and all associated data (in-app via Settings → Delete account, or via our account deletion request page)
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at dirkxmexx@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
